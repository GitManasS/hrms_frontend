SKIDOS is an educational technology startup that makes fun and challenging learning games. We make products that encourage thinking, solving and above all playing. SKIDOS learning games have been the top selling apps on the Apple AppStore for Kids with consumers in more than 40 countries. They have also won multiple awards including the silver award for the best mobile app for Children in the 2015 BMA Awards besides the 2015 Parents' Choice Fun Stuff Award and 2015 Family Choice Award.

We look forward to building more playful learning solutions for learning for young learners around the world.

We have built the “Netflix of learning games” for children aged 2-11 years to practice Math, coding and English skills by converting popular mobile games into learning games. These interactive learning games help parents prepare their children for a brighter future by making mathematics fun and reading enjoyable.
